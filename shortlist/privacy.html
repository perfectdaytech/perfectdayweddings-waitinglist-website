Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

PerfectDay ShortList ("the App") is operated by PerfectDay Technologies. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our venue comparison app.

2. Our Privacy-First Approach

PerfectDay ShortList is designed with privacy at its core. All your venue data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

3. Data We Store

  • All venue data, scores, questionnaire answers, and preferences are stored locally on your device using SQLite
  • We do not have access to your venue data
  • No account or login required
  • Data remains on your device unless you choose to export it

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google Places API Optional venue search Search queries only (via our gateway)
RevenueCat In-app purchase management Anonymous purchase receipts
Sentry Error reporting Anonymous crash reports (no personal data)
Apple App Store / Google Play App distribution & payments Payment info handled by Apple/Google

5. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data (unless you use venue search)
  • No usage patterns or behavioural data

6. Data Export & Deletion

  • Export all your data as JSON or CSV at any time via Settings
  • Delete all data from within the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

7. In-App Purchases

  • Subscriptions are managed through Apple App Store or Google Play
  • We use RevenueCat to verify purchase status
  • Payment information is handled entirely by Apple/Google
  • We do not store credit card or payment details

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us
